Our Mission

Climate change has surged in numerous deadly circumstances around the world. In recent years, It has greatly disturbed the habitats of many species, including invasive species. Invasive species are one of the greatest threats to biodiversity, land, and agricultural porductivity. Our mission is to predict the growth and locations of detrimental species. We aim to alert farmers, citizens, and officials to take action prior. With the help of our predictions we form a stroger healther land.
